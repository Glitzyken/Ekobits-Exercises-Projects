ðŸ“… Date: November 3, 2020

ðŸ—º Location: Vancouver, Canada

VanHack is doing a Virtual Hiring Event to connect the best DevOps professionals with some of Canada and Europe's fastest-growing companies.

If you are a DevOps looking to get hired abroad, that's the opportunity you were looking for.

What candidates are we looking for?

  • 4+ years of experience in a DevOps and/or SRE role
  • Experience with Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud Platform or Microsoft Azure
  • Experience with orchestration tools like Docker, Kubernetes
  • Experience in scripting languages
  • Familiar with Linux, Scrum and Agile methodologies
  • Eagerness to learn and share your knowledge
  • Bachelor Degree in Computer Science or related
  • Verified English on your VanHack Profile (Advanced or Fluent)

Nice to have: Good results in the coding challenge

The Event Process

  • Application for the event starts now and we recommend you complete all the mandatory steps (profile and English) within one week after your application. The faster you do it, the higher your chances to be reviewed by a hiring company
  • VanHack will accept companies and job descriptions until November 20th
  • According to the job requirements, VanHack recruiters will analyze your profile and if your skills and experience are matching, we will share your profile with the companies

Preparation

  • Candidates will receive Preparation such as classes, webinars and online courses
  • Some companies will be available for live Q&A sessions to answer all your questions.

Logistics during the event

  • If a company selects your profile, we will inform you immediately and will schedule the interview
  • Company Interviews will be held from November 23rd - 11th December
  • You will be informed about each interview [Mock and Actual] at least 48 hours in advance

If you have any questions, send an email to contact@vanhack.com.
